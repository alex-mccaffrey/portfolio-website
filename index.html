Industries to Impact

Ski Industry

As ski resorts adapt to these unique times, they are faced with new restraints, requirements, and unknowns. New technologies are immergeing, and will continue to immerge, to allow these resorts to operate at maximum relative efficiency, while providing guests an experince that is seemingly unchanged.

Search and Rescue

Search and Rescue organizations across the country live in the unkown. On top of that, a majority operate on a volunteer basis, relying on the goodwill of other for manpower, and donations for essential equipment. Well thought-out technology can not only save SAR organizations time and money, but could possibly save lives.

Avalanche Safety

Snow safety is a not just a big product industry, it is a moral responsibility. It is important to promote, teach, learn, and practice snow safety and responsibility. Creating applications, web resources, and other technology that is easily accessible, easy to navigate, and easy to obtain, can leave to a greater knowledge base and overall safety in the backcountry.
